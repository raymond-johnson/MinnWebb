FAQ

Answers to the stuff clients usually ask before they buy.

How long does a website take?

A one-page site can be done in a few days. A 5-page business site usually takes 1–2 weeks, depending on how quickly you provide photos/text and feedback.

Do you do domains and hosting?

I can help you connect a domain (yourbusiness.com). Hosting can be set up on a simple platform like Vercel/Netlify or traditional hosting if you prefer.

Can I update the site later?

Yes. I can build it so updates are easy, or you can request changes from me. If you want ongoing edits, I can offer a maintenance option.

What do you need from me to start?

Business name, services list, preferred colors (if any), a few photos (or I can use placeholders), and any examples of websites you like.
