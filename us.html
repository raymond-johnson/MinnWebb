Brady

I am a college student in Minnesota and the founder of MinnWebb Design. I started MinnWebb to help small businesses build, launch, and host websites that make it easier for people to find them, trust them, and grow with them.

Why MinnWebb

I started MinnWebb because I saw how many small businesses had strong products, services, and ideas but did not always have a website that matched the quality of what they offered. A good website should help a business feel more real, more trusted, and easier for customers to connect with.

My goal is to build, launch, and host websites that help businesses grow, whatever that business may be. I want every project to feel useful, thoughtful, and built around the real people behind it.

How We Work

  • Clear communication I keep the process simple and collaborative, with clear communication from start to finish.
  • Clean design I focus on building websites that look professional, feel modern, and represent the business well.
  • Useful websites The goal is not just to make a site look good, but to help the business grow and attract more customers.

What Clients Can Expect

I want clients to feel confident in their website, proud to share it, and excited about how it represents their business and ours. I also want them to know the site can keep evolving, with changes and updates available even after launch.
